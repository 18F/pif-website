Projects

Presidential Innovation Fellows work on a wide variety of projects during their twelve-month tenure, resulting in multiple opportunities to serve citizens through far reaching or hyper-focused efforts. Projects can be solely focused on supporting agencies, delivering on Presidential priorities, and/or delivering technology that has a cross-agency impact on the federal government. In general, the program focuses on three types of portfolio projects.

GOOD FOR GOVERNMENT
One of the focuses of the program is to help agencies bridge the technology gap between current government IT solutions and the private industry. To better serve citizens, Fellows work on products that can be utilized across multiple agencies to better serve the country.
PRESIDENTIAL AND AGENCY PRIORITIES
At times the President or agency leaders identify priorities to better serve citizens. The fellows will partner with agencies and the White House Office of Science and Technology, Policy to address these priorities through innovative approaches and solutions.
TECHNOLOGY PARTNERSHIP
As agencies develop new technology solutions and practices, guidance by outside industry leaders help support these efforts. Details to the agencies enable Fellows to closely collaborate with government partners.
